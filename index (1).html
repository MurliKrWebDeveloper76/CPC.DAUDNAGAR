About Concepts Classes (CPC)

Founded by Abhishek Raj

With over 5+ years of experience, Abhishek Raj established CPC to empower students in Daudnagar, Bihar. His vision is to provide world-class education for IIT-JEE, NEET, NIFT, Olympiads, NTSE, and Board exams.

CPC enrolls 800+ students annually, delivering top results in 10th and 12th Boards and competitive exams. Our motto, "Discipline is the Key to Success", drives our student-centric approach.

Equipped with CCTV surveillance, modern classrooms, and hands-on science experiments, CPC ensures a safe and engaging learning environment.
